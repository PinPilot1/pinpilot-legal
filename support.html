Support

Golf PinPilot

Need help with Golf PinPilot? We're happy to assist. Email us at pinpilot@hotmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

Common questions

• Distances or weather aren't showing. Make sure you've granted location permission to the app (iOS Settings → Golf PinPilot → Location) and that you have an internet connection.

• My course isn't mapped. Golf PinPilot's greens, tees and pars are mapped by the community. You can help map your home course from within the app, and we review submissions before they go live.

• Something looks wrong on a course. Use the report option in the app to flag it, and we'll review the data.

• I want to delete my account. Go to Settings → Delete account in the app. This permanently erases your profile, rounds, friends and clips.

Contact us

For anything else — questions, feedback, bug reports or feature requests — email pinpilot@hotmail.com. We read every message.
